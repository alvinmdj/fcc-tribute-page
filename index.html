Hajime Isayama

Creator of the Popular Series Attack on Titan

Here is the works of Hajime Isayama :

  • One-shot version of Attack on Titan (進撃の巨人 Shingeki no Kyojin) in year 2006.
  • One-shot manga Heart Break One (ハート ブレイク ワン Hāto Bureiku Wan) in year 2008.
  • One-shot manga Orz in year 2008.
  • Attack on Titan (進撃の巨人 Shingeki no Kyojin) manga series between year 2009-2021.
  • Attack on Titan Inside (進撃の巨人 INSIDE 抗 Shingeki no Kyojin Inside Kou) in year 2013.
  • Attack on Titan Outside (進撃の巨人 OUTSIDE 攻 Shingeki no Kyojin OUTSIDE Osamu) in year 2013.
  • Attack on Avengers, a crossover with Marvel Comics in year 2014.
  • The Killing Pawn (ザ・キリング・ポーン Za Kiringu Pōn) in year 2014.

“The only thing we're allowed to believe is that we won't regret the choice we made.” ― Hajime Isayama
